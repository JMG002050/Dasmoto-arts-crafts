1. Header Font:
   - Name: Fredoka
   - Style: Rounded, playful
   - Use for: Titles, headers, logos
   - CSS: font-family: 'Fredoka', sans-serif;
   - Google Fonts Link: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Fredoka

2. Body Font:
   - Name: Open Sans
   - Style: Clean, modern, easy to read
   - Use for: Body text, paragraphs, buttons, form labels
   - CSS: font-family: 'Open Sans', sans-serif;
   - Google Fonts Link: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans

3. Accent / Quote Font:
   - Name: Shadows Into Light
   - Style: Handwritten, casual
   - Use for: Quotes, testimonials, personal notes
   - CSS: font-family: 'Shadows Into Light', cursive;
   - Google Fonts Link: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Shadows+Into+Light
